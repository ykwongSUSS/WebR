4.2 Div Block - css and padding

\(\textbf{Gosset's Theorem}\)

If \(X_1, X_2, \ldots, X_n\) is a random sample from a \(N(\mu, \sigma)\) distribution, then the rv

\[ \frac{\bar X - \mu}{S/\sqrt{n}} \]

has the t distribution with (n-1) degrees of freedom, \(t_{n-1}.\)
